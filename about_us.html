About

Welcome to CX Community Africa

We are committed to empowering African businesses and professionals on matters concerning customer experience. Our challenges and strengths are unique to us. Let us collaborate to make a customer centric world. Just imagine a world where customers are happy and comes win!

Who we are

CX COMMUNITY AFRICA is a learning, informative and collaborative online community for Customer experience personnel, HR, Client Management, UX designers, Business Owners and Hospitality managers across Africa.
The community focuses on customer experience and client management specifically in Africa, putting into consideration the environment and culture, economic stability, government, policies and every other thing that makes up the region and affects its consumers.

Who is the CX community for?

The CX Community is targeted specifically to those who are;

  • Fully employed
  • Business owners
  • Start ups
  • Sponsors (you want to sponsor members of your workforce)

Our Benefits

Providing unlimited CX information



Join our CX Community and learn best practice on: customer support, customer thinking, measuring customer success, CX business analysis and others. By joining, you gain:

Access to new ways of solving problems, taking advantage of opportunities and accelerating your career.
Access to newsletters, invite to webinars and community forum where you can chat, ask us questions and get feedback.
Weekly Insights on client and customer related issues across Africa. If CX is important to you, why limit yourself to one source for latest news and insights?
Access to hybrid events hosted by the community for networking and meeting potential partners.

Why Should you join the CX Community Africa



CX Community Africa is a network of individuals and organisations committed to improving the customer experience in businesses around Africa.


Be a part of the most valuable and exclusive online community for Customer Experience enthusiasts.
By joining the community you will learn how to approach customer experience, how to measure program effectiveness and how to incorporate customer interactions into every aspect of your company's value chain.




After all - If you join the CX Community Africa for an entire year and benefit from our networking events, masterminds, masterclasses, case studies, and access to our content vault that will help you grow your CX, personal and profesional skills - all this for is for less than the cost of a single online CX exams or courses!
