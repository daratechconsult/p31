Join the Community

. About Us

The CX Community Africa is a learning, informative and collaborative virtual community for business owners and anyone who is in charge of the success of customers and their experience. The  community focuses on building members on understanding the customer, their experiences and management specifically in Africa, putting into consideration the environment and culture, economic, government,  policies and every other thing that makes up the region and affects its consumers.

Our Community Goals

Build

Building people in business or professionals in any industry to lead customer-centric ways

Equip

Filling the knowledge gap by equipping members with resources

Connect

Solid connections and networking with other professionals and businesses across Africa

Share Information

Keep members informed and updated on all things concerning CX

Support

Community support in many ways i.e. success tracking, and personalized services
