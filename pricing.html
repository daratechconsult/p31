CX means Customer Experience

To subscribe to our community all you have to do is join our mailing list. If you would like to get exclusive benefits, we have a paid plan. See our pricing page here for further details.

If you are fully employed, run a business, starting from scratch, or you are being sponsored/sponsoring an employee in your organisation, this is for you.

We do not refund payments already made. However, you can cancel your subscription before the next billing date.

To upgrade to an exclusive member, you would need to login to your account then proceed to click on the upgrade button and follow the instructions given.

Our payment platform i.e Paystack sends a payment reminder some days before your next billing date. At the bottom of this reminder, there is a button that says cancel subscription. You can click on it to cancel your subscription

Some of our members receive sponsorship to join from their employer as part of their employer’s commitment to employee personal development.
